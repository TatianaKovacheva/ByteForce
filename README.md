# 🧠 Project Overview

Problem: Math often seems complicated, boring, and stressful. Students lose motivation when they struggle with obscure formulas and voluminous homework, without access to fast and engaging help.

Our solution: We, ByteForce, create an interactive e-school that turns dry theory into a series of practical tests and self-study tasks. Through gamification and accessible explanations, the platform helps teens learn at their own pace and see real progress.

Goal: To transform math from a “scary obstacle” into an easy and engaging challenge, making academic achievement accessible to all.
